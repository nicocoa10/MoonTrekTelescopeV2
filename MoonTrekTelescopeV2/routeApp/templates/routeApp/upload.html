Welcome to Moon Trek Telescope Guide

Submit your own images of the moon and recieve useful information within the field of view
Begin by selecting your image of the moon on the right . For best registration results consider :

- Images of the moon with at least 50% of moon

- Clear , non blurry images

- Moon images (This can be from a telescope , camera , etc. as long as it meets above criteria)

Dont have images of the moon ? Go to the end of the guide for additional content...
Now you should be ready to click upload. Our application will attempt image registration. Things to consider:

- If image registers properly , we are able to annotate your image

- The next page will allow you to learn further details of your moon's view

- If registration test fails , then you will have to resubmit a better image

Dont have images of the moon ? Go to the end of the guide for additional content...
Go back in the slideshow if you still want to upload , go to next unless you dont have images to upload:

1. Begin by selecting your image of the moon

2. click upload

Go to next for additional content...
If you dont have images of the moon to use, you could still test some features of our application in a generic 3D model of the moon Go to Generic 3D Model
